테이블: 표를 만들 때 사용하는 태그이다.

```
<!doctype html>
<html>
    <body>
        <table border="1">
            <tr>
                <th> First  name</th>
                <th> Last name</th>
                <th> Score</th>
            </tr>
            <tr> 
            <td> Jill</td>
            <td> Smith</td>
            <td>50</td>
             </tr>
             <tr>
                <td>Eve</td>
                <td>Jackson</td>
                <td> 80</td>
             </tr>
        </table>
    </body>
</html>
```
